How It Works

  1. Enter your Discord webhook URL in the field below
  2. The QR code will be generated automatically
  3. When someone scans the QR code, it will capture their IP address and device information
  4. This information will be sent to your Discord channel via the webhook
  5. You will receive a message with the user's IP, location, and device details
